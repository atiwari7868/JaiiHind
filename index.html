Bhagat Singh

Biography

Bhagat Singh was an Indian freedom fighter, known for his courageous actions against British colonial rule. His patriotism and sacrifices continue to inspire generations Born on September 28, 1907, in Punjab, India, he was deeply influenced by the injustices of British rule from a young age. Joining the Hindustan Socialist Republican Association, he engaged in revolutionary activities aimed at achieving complete independence for India.

Born: September 28, 1907
Died: March 23, 1931
