Biography

Shaheed Bhagat singh (born c. 28 September 1907, Lyallpur, western Punjab, India August died March 23, 1931, Lahore [now in Pakistan])Bhagat Singh was an Indian anti-colonial revolutionary, who participated in the mistaken murder of a junior British police officer in December 1928 in what was to be retaliation for the death of an Indian nationalist.
